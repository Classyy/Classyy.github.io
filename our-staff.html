Our Staff

Sue Foley (Director)

Sue is the principal and director of Landley Law. She has extensive experience, having practised law for the last 10 years. She prides herself on providing a personalised and high standard of customer service to her clients. Sue aims to be easy to contact, accessible and responsive to her client's needs. She has great communication skills and an ability to explain complicated legal matters to clients, by providing realistic examples. Sue has the back-up of a great team who all share her values regarding outstanding client service.
